About

Hi, I'm Markus. My whole professional life I dedicated to developing great IT solutions in several industies. For every project my goal is always to create a best possible product which serves the user as best as it can and in an quality which helps my customers later to maintain and extend this. Therefore the whole IT lifecycle needs to be considered.

Every team member is an important part of the product/project success, no matter if business or IT side. My broad knowledge of working in different industries like automotive, legal and advertising is helping me to understand various business needs. Also my experiences in most of the IT areas, like product management, software developing, business analysing and testing, helps me to solve issues in different part of the software lifecycle and work as good as possible with all kinds of experts.

Philosophy

Process oriented

I convinced that great products comes with great processes, ether business process or developing process. My work is still focused on how does the product fits into the business processes of their end users.

Improve people's daily life

I'm pretty sure IT is some good. The whole information technologies are so powerful why not using them to create software which helps people in their daily life. I'm convinced that great products which are supporting real needs people in their daily life also are helping your company in grow.

Less is sometimes more

I always think that a clean and less complex design brings more value and joy to your users. This is an important part that users like to use the product more often and on a regular base.
